WELCOME TO POKER: Texas Hold'em

About

Texas hold 'em is one of the most popular variants of poker. Each player receives two cards, and five community cards are dealt face up in three stages.

Step 2

Flop

Flop: The first three community cards are dealt face-up in the center of the table. This is followed by another round of betting. Players now have five cards to consider (their two hole cards plus the three community cards).
