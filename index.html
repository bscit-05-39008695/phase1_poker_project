WELCOME TO POKER: Texas Hold'em

About

Texas hold 'em (also known as Texas holdem, hold 'em, and holdem) is one of the most popular variants of the card game of poker. Two cards, known as hole cards, are dealt face down to each player, and then five community cards are dealt face up in three stages.All players in the game use these shared community cards in conjunction with their own hole cards to each make their best possible five-card poker hand.
